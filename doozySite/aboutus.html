Our Planet

We are on a mission to ensure that Doozy holds the highest sustainability standards. This means choosing local suppliers, efficient ways of working and recycling efforts. We are going a step further this year, aiming for a completely (single-use) plastic-free vending solution. Watch this space…

Our Charity

We have shared our office space and resources with EdUKaid since 2003. The charity, based in South-East Tanzania, aims to improve the educational prospects of some of the countries poorest children. Doozy provides essential support, as well as funds from our profits. We’ve grown together for over 15 years.
